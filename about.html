History of Madhvi Handlooms

The history of the state speaks of the impeccable beauty of Patan’s Patola, widely known for the true craftsmanship and beautiful design. Non the less, the craft is suffering. Dates back to 900 years ago, 700 weavers were brought to Patan, there they began weaving as a community, but today the only weavers left is the Salvi family and the Soni family. Salvi family don’t appreciate new commers into the weavers community, I suspect, it might have been a strategic move to keep the productions low to be able to charge more. Certainly they aren’t on talking terms, Salvi family had the skills and resources as its heritage, where Soni family took notice of the dying craft and brought it back to life.
