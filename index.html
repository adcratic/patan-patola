Significance of Patola
Pidi Ceremony

Patola are so pure in nature that it keeps its strength and beauty even after ages transitioning from one generation to another. There’s a saying in Patan, Gujarat that speaks about the strength of the fabric “Padi Patel Bhat, Fitta bhi nahi”.
